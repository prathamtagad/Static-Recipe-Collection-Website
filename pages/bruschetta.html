Bruschetta
Details

Bruschetta is a traditional Italian appetizer featuring slices of toasted bread topped with a fresh mixture of diced tomatoes, basil, garlic, and olive oil. Often served as a starter, itâ€™s light, flavorful, and easy to make. Sometimes, balsamic glaze or mozzarella is added for extra taste.

Instructions

    Prep the Tomatoes:

  • Dice the tomatoes and place them in a bowl.
  • Add chopped basil, salt, pepper, and olive oil.
  • Mix and let it sit for 10-15 minutes for flavors to meld.

  • Place the bread slices on a tray. Toast for 5â€“7 minutes until golden and crisp.

    • Assemble the Bruschetta:

  • Spoon the tomato-basil mixture onto each toasted bread slice.
  • Drizzle with extra olive oil or balsamic glaze if desired.
